FAQ.

What is offside?

A player is offside if they are closer to the opponent’s goal line than both the ball and the second-last defender.

Who is considered the greatest football player of all time?

This is a highly debated topic, with many considering players like Pelé, Maradona, Cristiano Ronaldo and Messi among the greatest.

What is a penalty kick?

A penalty kick is awarded for serious fouls within the penalty area. It’s a one-on-one opportunity for a player to score against the goalkeeper.

What is a yellow card and a red card?

A yellow card is a warning for a minor foul. A red card is a more serious punishment, resulting in the player being sent off the field.
